Who We Are

We're Shababeek Labs — a small team from Egypt building VR stuff we actually care about. "Shababeek" means "Windows" in Arabic, and that's exactly what we do: open windows into other realities. Games, training simulations, AR events, education, metaverse worlds — if it involves putting on a headset and going somewhere else, we're probably already building it.

We're based in Egypt with presence in the UAE. No massive team, no corporate fluff — just five people who think VR is cooler than reality and are stubborn enough to prove it.

Good Morning, { employee name }

Genre: VR Dark Comedy
Platform: Meta Quest & PCVR
Status: In Development
Style: Cassette Futurism

A dark comedy VR experience set in a corporate office that shouldn't exist. Cassette futurism meets workplace horror. 12 days of existential office weirdness where nothing is quite right and the employee manual keeps changing.
